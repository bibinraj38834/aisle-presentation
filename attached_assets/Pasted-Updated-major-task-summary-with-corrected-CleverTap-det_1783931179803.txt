Major Tasks - Aisle
Task	Status	Feature Summary
Clevertap Integration	In Progress	Integrates CleverTap as a new analytics and engagement destination. Aisle and Notification services produce required user events through Kafka, while the Events Tracking service consumes them and handles CleverTap delivery. Scope includes login, onboarding/profile completion, profile views, likes, passes, comments, matches, payment events, purchase events, received interactions, account deletion, and feature-limit events.
Date Feedback	In Prod	Adds a private post-match feedback flow where users can share whether they met a match and whether they would meet again. This helps measure real-world dating outcomes, improve future recommendations, and create trust/safety signals from repeated negative feedback.
SmartPicks Aisle	Complete	Introduces curated Smart Picks for female users, showing relevant male profiles that need more exposure. The feature aims to improve like distribution, especially for recently joined underexposed male users, while maintaining recommendation relevance for women.
Major Tasks - Arike
Task	Status	Feature Summary
Socket on Vernacs	In Prod	Implements real-time socket updates for Vernac/Arike so users no longer need to manually refresh for new matches, incoming messages, unread counts, chat ordering, and in-app nudges. The socket service consumes Kafka events and supports refresh_matches and show_popup flows, with the foundation designed to support Vernac first and Aisle later.
MBG Arike	In Prod	Adds Money Back Guarantee for eligible Arike premium plans to reduce purchase hesitation. Users who buy an eligible plan and meet activity/profile requirements can claim a refund if they receive no match within the defined window.
Support Lounge for Arike	In Progress	Builds an Arike-specific Support Lounge inside Help Center with relevant icons, FAQs, and support content. The goal is to give users a structured place to resolve payment, premium, account, and feature-related queries.
For Vernacs specifically, the socket work also includes Boost Phase 2, which adds contextual Boost pop-ups for approved male users based on behavior cohorts like new users, DAU users, low visibility, likes sent, and profile edits. The goal is to improve Boost discovery, purchase conversion, engagement, and monetization through better-timed nudges.
